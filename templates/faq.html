Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

To register for the online library, click on the "Register" button on the homepage. Fill in your details and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email with a link to activate your account.

Once logged in, search for the book you want to borrow using the search bar. Click on the "Borrow" button on the book's detail page. The book will be added to your account under "My Borrowed Books."

You can borrow books for up to 14 days. After that, you will need to return the book or renew your loan to avoid late fees.
